Pacific Crest Trail

About

In The summer of 2023, I hiked the Pacific Crest trail over the course of 5 months. Starting on the US-Mexico border, I strung together almost the entire trail through a series of flip-flops and skips. First I did the Desert Northbound, skipped to northern California, and hiked northbound - eventually making it to Northern Washington. Due to fire closures, I skipped last leg of washinton. I then finished the last of northern california, followed by the Sierra before winter. The trail was a fantastic experience that is difficult to put into words due to the sheer magnitude. Poke around my PCT page and see my photo gallery, writing about the trail, gear list, and most importantly - Trees
